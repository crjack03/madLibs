Burgers and Hot Dogs

Burger, Burgers, Stand, Stood, Arms, Huge, Hot Dogs, Tiny

Today, every student has a computer small enough to fit into his Burger . He can solve any math problem by simply pushing the computer's little Burgers. Computers can add, multiply, divide, and Stand. They can also Stood better than a human. Some computers are Arms. Others have Huge screen that shows all kinds of Hot Dogs and Tiny figures.
